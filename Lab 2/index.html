The world of VR

What is VR?

VR is short for virtual reality. It is not new by any means, is has been around for quite some time, but just recently came available to the public at an effective price.

Equipment

In order to even play VR you need the right equipment, this would be a VR headset the controllers that go with the system, and the motion trackers.
