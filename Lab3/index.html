Intro into VR

VR can be expensive but it opens a new world to you. You can do things, go places, and experence things you never could in your daily life.

There are three systems I would recommend and there are as follows:

  • Oculus Rift
  • HTC Vive
  • Valve Index

Expectations

People expect to jump right into the deep end of the pool and enjoy the world of Ready Player One, we are sadly not there yet. There is still a difference between the worlds, but in time you will adjust and start to feel at home in VR.
